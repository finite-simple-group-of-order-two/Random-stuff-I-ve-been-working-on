Here I dump what prob1, prob2, prob3 etc are about

prob1.py:
Program to find numbers under 100 that are divisible by either 3 or 5

prob2.py:
Program to find sum of even terms in a Fibonacci series that are less
than or equal to 4 million

prob3.py:
Intended objective: Find greatest prime factor of a very large number
Result: Scrubbed because algorithm took too long for decently large numbers

prob3_1.1.py
Program to find greatest prime factor of a very large number, accomplished using
sympy package.

prob4.py
Program to find out largest palindrome number that is a product of two 3-digit numbers
